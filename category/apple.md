<P>Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. It is considered one of the Big Four tech companies along with Amazon, Google, and Facebook.</P>

<p>Apple Currently Releases Various Products Such as Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Apple TV, Homepod and Softwares for their Products Such as MacOS, iOS, WatchOS and tvOS.</p>
